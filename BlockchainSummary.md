   POLYGON_RPC_URL=https://rpc-mumbai.maticvigil.com

   USDT_MUMBAI=0x326C977E6efc84E512bB9C30f76E30c160eD06FB

4. Send test transaction to your deposit address

5. Monitor should detect it within 30 seconds

6. Confirmations are faster on testnet (Mumbai: ~30 confirms)
